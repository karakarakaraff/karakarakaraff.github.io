Newspaper design

In my career with newspapers, I had the pleasure of working with several respected publications, including the Denver Post, Boulder Daily Camera, St. Paul Pioneer Press and several more. I earned my degree in journalism with an emphasis in mass media, but of all the mediums where journalism exists, I've always been especially drawn to newspapers. I was the editor of both my high school and university newspapers, and I've worked professionally on both the editorial and marketing sides. (Side note: The editorial side is my favorite.)

You can learn more about my newspaper career on LinkedIn. Although I've left newspapers behind for a career in tech, I remain extremely passionate about the state of journalism and the survival of quality print media in communities of all sizes.
